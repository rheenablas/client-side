Instructions

Avoid the ghosts and save the townspeople! Use arrow keys/WASD to navigate.

Once the health is running low, manna will fall from heaven, collect it before it falls off the canvas! Regenerate health in the sanctuary on the middle right side of the canvas (the little platform).

Spacebar to turn the ghosts neutral and defeat the enemies! Firing at the enemies in the sanctuary is disabled.

Cheats

  • Press Z to increase player speed. Press X to decrease player speed.
  • Press C to spawn enemies. Press V to remove enemies.

Game Lore

As the world comes to its end, people are in a state of panic, not knowing what to do. Your job is to save the people by sharing the good news that all may have everlasting peace, eternal life. “He said to them, ‘Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation.” Mark 16:15

The enemies are going to come at you no matter what you do, so shoot them with love to neutralize them. But know that even as you sow your love to them, their purpose is to bring you down. “But I tell you, love your enemies and pray for those who persecute you, that you may be children of your Father in heaven.” Matthew 5:44

As you suffer from the persecutions of those whose eyes are closed off to the truth, manna (bread) from heaven will come down to strengthen you. Take it and be satisfied. Spend some time in the sanctuary to replenish your strength and be free from the attacks of the enemies.
